Refinance Comparison

How the mortgage math works:

Your loan is paid off by making the same monthly payment until the balance reaches zero. Each month the amounts are updated using these simple formulas:
interest = balance * rate,
principal_paid = payment - interest,
new_balance = balance - principal_paid.
